What is Pic-A-Talk

a communication-aide for non-verbal kids

Pic-A-Talk is a communication-aide specially created for verbally-challenged children, usually low-functioning autistic kids. These children think in pictures, they learn better when they see an image associated with the word, so this application contains images of things children are usually interested in as well as an audio for each image. If a child is interested in an item, they click it and an audio associated with that object is played. Not only does this help them in communicating with other people, it is also a learning experience since they are exposed to the word and image. Every click is then recorded for the parent's further monitoring of their child's activities.
